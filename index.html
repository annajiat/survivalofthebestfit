Machine Bias Exists Everywhere

Is software neutral?

Survival of the Best Fit is an educational game about hiring bias in AI. We aim to explain how the misuse of AI can make machines inherit human biases and further inequality. Much of the public debate on AI has presented its as a threat imposed on us, rather than one that we have agency over. We want to change that by helping people understand the technology, and demand more accountability from those building increasingly pervasive software systems.

Supported by the Creative Media Award from Mozilla Foundation.

Built by Gabor, Jihyun, Miha, and Alia.
